Highland Whispers

Uncover the Past, Solve the Mystery.

Designer, Developer and creator: Diana Kravchuk

Faculty: Creative industry

I am a developer and designer with a passion for creating immersive, story-driven experiences that take players on a journey through beautifully crafted worlds. With a background in both web development and design, I strive to build games that blend engaging narratives with exploration and discovery. My latest project, Highland Whispers, is a reflection of my love for folklore, history, and open-world gameplay.

As the founder of World Outside Studios, my goal is to bring players into worlds that feel alive, filled with secrets and stories waiting to be uncovered. I believe that games have the power to transport us, not just to new places but into new perspectives, sparking curiosity and a sense of adventure. At World Outside Studios, we focus on creating games that encourage exploration, puzzle-solving, and storytelling, while also delivering beautiful, visually striking environments. With Highland Whispers, we are telling a deeply personal story about family, history, and the mysteries hidden in our past. I’m excited to continue developing experiences that engage players’ minds and imaginations, while always pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in indie game development.
